MY FRIEND

Theoretically, friendship is a bond shared by two peer individuals between them. However, in practice, the concept of friendship is generally broader and broader. It includes in its domain a number of diverse emotions, feelings and sentiments. There is nothing like a good friend or a good friend or a perfect friend. This concept is very subjective, and the level of friendship varies from person to person.

Throughout our lives, we meet many people. Some of them remained, just acquaintances, while others eventually became some of our close friends. People who are very close become like family away from family to us. Some say, a good friend is a family member of our own choosing; he is very precise.

Friendship is built primarily on two pillars of love and trust. From these two emotions flow other intermediary feelings between friends. A good and loyal friend supports you, supports you, and accompanies you through your hard and happy times.

Like me, I have many friends where I find it difficult to choose which one is the best because all of them are the best. Thank you for giving me such good things in friendship.They are the best friends I have ever had.
